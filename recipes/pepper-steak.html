Pepper Steak

(Recipe pilfered from Recipe Tin Eats.)

Description

A wicked pepper steak. Kind of boozy.

Serves well with both kinds of music, Country and Western.

Not to be confused with Pepper Steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Peppercorns
  • Brandy
  • Beef broth
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the steak
  2. Set the steak aside
  3. Cook the brandy and broth
  4. Stir in the cream and peppercorns
  5. Pour the sauce over the steak
