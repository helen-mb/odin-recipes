Toast

Description

Some dry white toast.

White bread, toasted.

Ingredients

  • A slice of white bread
  • Another slice of white bread (if you're feeling wild)
  • Heat source - e.g. toaster, electric coil, gas range, hair dryer

Steps

  1. Take the slice of bread and place it on or near the heat source
  2. Watch it while waiting
  3. (Optional) Check the slice for colour
  4. (Optional) Flip the slice
  5. Decide when the colour is satisfactory
  6. Remove the toast
