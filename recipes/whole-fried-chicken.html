Whole Fried Chicken

Description

Whole fried chicken. Food for the soul.

Pairs well with a Coke

Ingredients

  • A whole chicken
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Measure out the oil
  2. Dry the chicken
  3. Perforate the bird
  4. Rub it down with spices
  5. Put it in a basket
  6. Take it out of the basket
  7. Put it in the deep fry basket
  8. Heat the oil
  9. Put the bird in the oil
  10. Watch and wait for 20-30 minutes
  11. Remove the bird
  12. Let it sit for 10 minutes while repeating with three more chickens
